Bish-Bosh 2.0

A variant of FizzBuzz. Enter two numbers Bish and Bosh, and how far the sequence will go (starts with 1). Sequence entries that are divisible by Bish, Bosh or both will have their value replaced by the corresponding word ("Bish", "Bosh", or "BishBosh") Only positive integers.
